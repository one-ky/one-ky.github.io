About Me

I am a Data Scientist with two years of experience at Johnson and Johnson. I have direct work experience with statistical analysis and machine learning for large pharmaceutical datasets. Projects I have worked on include XGBoost and Elastic Net Regression for small cell cancer drug research as well as EDA for similar datasets. My work consists of python programming the packages statsmodels, sci-kit learn, seaborn, matplotlib, and tensorflow. For personal projects I have explored MLOps and LLMs. I have quantized models to run on personal hardware as well as A100 GPUs in Google Collab. I have also implemented LoRA fine-tuning techniques to optimize model training, reducing computational resources while maintaining performance. I have a passion for learning and keeping up to data with common ML and AI practices and love to build solutions to problems.
