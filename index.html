About Me

I'm a creative Data Analyst with a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and over 6 months of experience looking to expand my interests into Data Science and Data Analytics. During my time as a Data Analyst, I helped my team clean and process data that was used in accreditation reports, data visualization, and internal decision making. I've always been driven by development and problem solving. As a Data Analyst Intern, I was tasked with finding a way to parse through a survey to collect key software terms that companies are looking for in candidates in the job market today through the survey responses. After researching different techniques, I settled on a text analysis method that I wrote a script for in Python. I demonstrated trends and insight in the job market and impacted future strategies to develop coursework that prepares candidates for employment. After my completion of the internship, I personally studied neural networks utilizing the Fast AI Python library where I was able to implement basic neural networks and a Natural Language Processing project. My curiosity led me to explore a new and interesting field where I will continue to develop. Also, I have experience with collaborative efforts in a group environment. As a Data Analyst, I worked in a small group with other analyst. We would clarify the problem that needed to be addressed and align the best tools to meet the needs of the team. With over 30 players it was my duty to command the infield and orchestrate the defense of the team, understanding where some players are weak and need assistance and where their strength lie.
